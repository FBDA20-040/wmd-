Bulawayo Railway Museum

Consists of vintage trains from 1897, Cecil John Rhodes personal conveyance train being one of them. Also got photographs of British families and trains constructed by North British Locomotive company

Nesbitt Castle

It is European and centered in Bulawayo. Nesbit is remarkable in that its designer is unfamiliar awe-inspiring to those who come to view and admire the city's European feel. Due to it's stunning gardens people hold their celebration ceremonies/parties there

Khami Ruins

UNESCO has designed the ruins as a world heritage site. Tourists will see the Noble's and the ordinary's paddock.Constructions that were used to hold cattle are still visible. It is considered the most popular tourist attraction in Bulawayo.

National History Museum of Zimbabwe

It is found at Leopold Takawira Avenue, Centenary Park, Bulawayo. It is one of the top in Africa. Here tourists get to view and get knowledge about history of Zimbabwe, fauna treasury and excavation of minerals.

Shortage of water

Bulawayo was short of water since 1894, even to this point there is still that shortage water. The use of this water caused malaria out breaks.

Obstacles for low income housing issues and alternatives

Building for necessitous people has long been a main issue in Bulawayo. This failure has been the primary cause of the word's crowded settlement or village. Accordings to findings there are several good projects in Bulawayo to give housing to the disadvantaged. Despite their noble appearance, they stop well short of providing affordable homes for the vulnerable and prove to be a possible health threat for the region.
